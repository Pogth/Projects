How to Play:

1. Select the mode you want to play.

2. Try to guess the word by entering your guesses in the input field.

3. You have a limited number of attempts.

4. Green tiles indicate correct letters in the correct positions.

5. Yellow tiles indicate correct letters in the wrong positions.

6. Keep guessing until you either solve the word or run out of attempts!
